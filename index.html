IGBOBI
STUDENT ASSOCIATION

Igbobi College is a college established by the Anglican and Methodist Churches in 1932, in Yaba suburb of Lagos, Lagos State, South-Western Nigeria. It is still on its original site and most of the original buildings are intact. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious college in Nigeria, having produced many notable figures in the Nigerian professional and political scenery.
